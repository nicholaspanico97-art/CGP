# Opportunity Journal

Newest entries at top. Every considered opportunity gets an entry —
including the ones passed on (a "no trade" with reasoning is data too).
Entries are written BEFORE entry per RULESET.md; outcome filled in
after exit.

## Entry template

```
## [#NNN] YYYY-MM-DD — <asset / opportunity>
**Status:** considering | passed | open | closed
**Thesis:** <one or two sentences>
**What kills it:** <named downside / invalidation>
**FAT analysis:** <link or one-line summary of pros/cons discussion>
**Size:** $___  **Max loss:** $___
**Exit plan:** <target / invalidation level / time stop — set before entry>
**Entered:** <date, price>   **Exited:** <date, price>
**Outcome:** <P&L in $ and %>
**Lesson:** <what this entry taught, win or lose>
```
